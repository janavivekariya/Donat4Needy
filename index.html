About Donate4Needy

Donate 4 Needy forms a bridge between people who want to donate and the people who need help.

People blessed with food resources would be more than happy to help the people who need it. But maybe could not do it because of hectic schedule or safety measures due to Covid-19.

Through Donate 4 Needy you can conveniently donate Nutrition Kit and Education Kit from your home without going out.

Nutrition kit includes raw food materials and education kit includes stationary which is necessary for education of children.

This is an initiative by a student to eliminate hunger from society.

What we do

To donate through donate for the needy, you need to place the donation through our website.

 

After that we will donate the kits to needy people.

 

We will donate to people like children of low salaried maids, labourers.people in slum areas,orphanages,etc

This is a completely student run organisation.

Some of our recent work

Donation Drive on 26th January
Donate4Needy conducted a donation drive on 72nd Republic Day to form a bridge between people who want to donate and people who need help especially in these uncertain times of covid.During January 2021 India had less covid cases and government had allowed people to move out.So, we decided to go and help people who were affected by these health crisis due to loss of job and scarcity of basic resources.We conducted a donation drive where we collected raw food materials,clothes and stationary to donate it to people in need.We used social media platform to reach out to people who were interested in donating.We got a amazing response with a huge amount of things to donate.We donated to more than 500 people who were in need of basic resources in the times of difficulty.Seeing smile on faces of women and kids after receiving clothes,raw food materials,toys were worth for all the hard work and effort we did to connect people who wanted to donate and who needed help.

Some of our recent work

STEM Workshop
Donate4Needy conducted a STEM Activity and Awareness workshop to spread knowledge about STEM to underprivileged children.During April and May COVID Cases were on spike in India as India was going through 2nd wave of COVID-19.It was dangerous for us to move out of our house.So, we decided do something to spread positivity and knowledge in these uncertain times.We decided to contact some people at a nearby village of Ichhapore.We got a spectacular response of 30 kids who were excited to be a part of this Workshop.We taught kids about science with experiments using basic resources, taught about basic programming which kids really enjoyed and also about how mathematics evolved with time and how it is a integral part of our lives today.The kids were really excited and said that they really enjoyed the workshop and learned many things which they never knew.During this time kids had summer holidays but could not go to play due to covid and there was were negative emotions all around as many family members were suffering with health crisis.We tried to spread positivity by helping kids engage in these interesting maths and science which they could learn at their homes during their holidays.Seeing the excited and happy faces of kids and hearing their interesting conversations were a sign that we succeed in our mission to spread positivity and knowledge in these uncertain times.
